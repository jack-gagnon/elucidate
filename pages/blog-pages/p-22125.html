HERACLE

Mobile, and stationary, learning management platform.

The daily, rapid, creation of information is observable. There are now numerous sources available to students. A wide variety of perspectives - donated through various mediums.

This (hosted) iteration of the interface is primarily for our Y-Combinator application. Screenshots below illustrate the wireframe version of the prototype.

Fine tuning the features for the needs of teachers and students (K - 12+) is part of the ongoing initiative.

The final version will allow students to keep track of resources, resource-mediums; and, will allow educators to track resources used by students to personalize their program.

Similar to existing platforms - educators will be able to upload curated resources and quizes, and assign marks (/grades).

In the wake of COVID-19; mobile learning management systems seem to be even more relevant. Still - optimizing education, without damaging the current system, requires time, debate, and structured planning.
