Organizational Ergonomics

Leverage individual diversity and interior design to make your group hyperefficient.

Helping businesses navigate their internal operating environment to achieve efficiencies and increase productivity.

Behavioral differences between group members can be a detriment to the group's overall productivity. By analyzing these behaviors, and those of other groups internally, resources can be reallocated into more efficient groups that can boost the organizations overall productivity.

Our consultants have refined processes and visualization tools that illuminate the efficiencies and inefficiencies of certain group structures and hierarchies.

Research suggests simple changes in interior design - lighting, art, furniture, and furniture arrangement - can make the workday more enjoyable; indirectly, this leads to increased productivity and healthier collaboration.
